Your days matter. Pulse helps you capture them — moment by moment, day by day, and now week by month.

Pulse is a fast, beautiful journaling app built around one simple idea: log what's happening and how you feel about it, right when it happens. No lengthy prompts. No complicated setup. Just your moments, scored and saved.

CAPTURE YOUR DAY AS IT HAPPENS
Add log entries throughout the day — a quick note and a score from very negative to very positive. Pulse builds a picture of your day from the inside out.

REFLECT EVERY EVENING
End each day with a short reflection. Pulse keeps your daily summary alongside your moments so you always have the full picture.

SEE YOUR TIMELINE AT A GLANCE
The timeline bar chart shows every day at a glance — bar height and color reflect your average score. Scroll back through weeks and months of history in seconds.

WEEK & MONTH VIEWS
Zoom out and see the bigger picture. Browse your journal week by week or month by month. Each period shows a mini chart of your scores, and every day card beneath it reveals your moments in detail. Switch between Day, Week, and Month with a tap — Pulse remembers your choice.

PERSONALIZE YOUR EXPERIENCE
Choose from multiple color themes and set a background image from your photo library or our curated collection. Your journal should feel like yours.

STAY CONSISTENT WITH REMINDERS
Set multiple daily reminders to log throughout the day, plus an optional evening reminder to write your daily reflection.

PRIVATE AND ALWAYS WITH YOU
Your entries sync automatically across all your devices via iCloud — private, secure, and always up to date.
