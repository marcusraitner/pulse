Version 2.0

Pulse now lets you see the bigger picture.

Two new views give you a broader perspective on your days — without losing the detail that makes journaling meaningful.

WEEK VIEW
Browse your journal week by week. A mini bar chart shows your scores at a glance, and each day card beneath it reveals your moments at a compact but readable level of detail.

MONTH VIEW
Zoom out further and see your entire month at once. Day cards show a color-coded dot for each logged moment, so you can spot patterns across weeks in seconds.

Switch between Day, Week, and Month anytime using the new segmented control at the top — Pulse remembers your preference.

Also in this update:
- Tap any day card to open the reflection for that day
- Various scroll and layout improvements
